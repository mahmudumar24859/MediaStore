Sakon Maraba

Assalamu Alaikum Warahmatullah, 'yan uwa masoya manzan Allah (SAW) muna yi miku barka da zuwa wannan shafi namu mai albarka, munayiwa kowa da kowa fatan alheri, Allah ya bamu nasara a rayuwa duniya da lahira, Iyayenmu Allah yayi musu rahama ya albarkacesu a duniya da lahira.Manufar bude wannan shafi shine da farko neman yardar Allah ta hanyar ilmantar da al’umma addininsu da rayuwarsu, da zamantakewarsu da zaman lafiya tare da yimuku fatan alheri duniya da lahira. A takaice wannan shafi ya kunshi bangarori guda uku:

Bangare na Farko - Bangaren al’amuran yau da kullum.

Bangare na Biyu - Bangaren Taskar karatun malam, ta yadda kuna da damar saurare online da kuma damar saukewa zuwa wayoyinku, ko computer.

Bangare na Uku - Bangaren wallafa rubutu (blog), ta yadda ko wannenku yana da damar wallafa wani rubutu ko kuma karanta rubutun da waninsa yayi.

Duka dai don neman yardar Allah (SWT). Mungode Sheikh Abdulwahab Abdallah (Imamu Ahlussunnah Wal-Jama'a).
